## 2. What data we collect

Stackle uses third-party services that collect data on our behalf. We do not operate any server that stores your personal data directly. The data collected is the following.

### 2.1 Firebase Analytics (Google)

Firebase Analytics collects data about how users interact with the App. This includes:

- Pseudonymous installation identifier (Firebase Install ID)
- App events: game starts and ends, drops, milestones, ad interactions, in-app purchases
- Approximate location (country level), inferred from IP address (the IP address itself is not stored)
- Device model, Android operating system version, app version
- Language preference and device locale
- Session duration and first-open timestamp

This data is associated with the pseudonymous identifier and is not directly linked to your real identity unless you separately provide it (we do not ask).

### 2.2 Google AdMob

The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob collects:

- Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID, or AAID)
- IP address (used for ad serving and frequency capping)
- Approximate location, derived from IP address
- Interaction with advertisements (impressions, clicks, video reward completion)

When you grant consent through the in-app GDPR consent form, AdMob may serve **personalized ads** based on your interests. When consent is denied, AdMob serves **non-personalized ads** instead, which use only contextual data (no personal profile).

You can reset or disable your Advertising ID at any time through your Android device settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads).

### 2.3 Google Play Billing

In-app purchases (e.g. Stackle Pro) are processed by Google Play. **We do not access or store payment information.** Google Play handles all transaction data, including credit card details, billing address, and purchase history. The only purchase information visible to the App is whether you have acquired Stackle Pro.

### 2.4 Local data on your device

Stackle stores the following data **locally on your device only**, in Android SharedPreferences. This data is not transmitted to our servers (we have no servers) or to any third party:

- Your highest score
- Audio settings (sound on/off)
- Stackle Pro entitlement state (whether you have purchased Pro)
- Internal counters used for showing ads at appropriate intervals
- One-time event flags (used to fire each "first time" analytics event exactly once)

This local data is removed when you uninstall the App or clear app data through Android settings.

## 5. Your rights

If you are in the European Union, the United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with similar data protection laws, you have the following rights:

- **Right to access** — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Right to rectification** — you can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")** — you can request deletion of your data.
- **Right to restrict processing** — you can request that we limit how we use your data.
- **Right to data portability** — you can request a copy of your data in a portable format.
- **Right to object** — you can object to our processing of your data, including for direct marketing or based on legitimate interest.
- **Right to withdraw consent** — you can withdraw your consent at any time. To withdraw consent for analytics and personalized ads, you can:
  - Reset your Advertising ID in Android settings
  - Reinstall the App, which will re-trigger the GDPR consent form
  - Contact us at the email below to request data deletion

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **gsorge.apps@gmail.com**. We will respond within 30 days.

If you believe we have not addressed your request adequately, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In Italy, this is the **Garante per la protezione dei dati personali** (https://www.gpdp.it).

### California residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including:
- The right to know what personal information we collect about you
- The right to request deletion of your personal information
- The right to opt out of the "sale" of your personal information (we do not sell your data)
- The right to non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights

To exercise CCPA rights, contact us at the email above.
